AISTATS*2014 Registration

The registration site for AISTATS is now open. click here to fill in the registration form. Hotel booking is done through the same form. The early registration deadline is 21 February 2014.

Registration Fees

Student early registration: 250 EUR
Student late registration: 350 EUR
Regular early registration: 500 EUR
Regular late registration: 600 EUR


Visa support letters can only be sent to people who have completed the registration process. Send your request to congress@congress.is with the following information:
  • Your name as it appears on your passport
  • The name of the conference you wish to attend
  • Your registration confirmation number
For more information on visa requirements for visiting Iceland, see http://www.utl.is/index.html?option=com_content&view=article&id=16&Itemid=17&lang=en.

Local Contact Email Address

The local contact email address for AISTATS 2014 is aistats@iiim.is.
